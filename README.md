# UniBring

UniBring is a university-only peer assistance platform that connects day scholars with in-campus hostel students to bring permitted items into campus. The app focuses on safety, accountability, and university compliance without handling real money.

Hostel students often face difficulty accessing essential items due to campus restrictions, while day scholars already travel in and out daily. UniBring bridges this gap by enabling trusted, student-to-student assistance within a closed university ecosystem.

The platform ensures fairness and safety through request locking, proof-based delivery, and a virtual campus credit system.
